Return Policies:
1.Commodity follows the "7 days no reason to return" policy: Since the buyer within 7 days from the date of receipt of the courier, the goods and the goods themselves are kept in their original shape at the time of sale and the accessory gifts information is complete, which does not affect the secondary sales and there is no reason to return the goods.
2.The return of goods caused by commodity quality problems or the wrong reasons of the merchants, etc., shall be entirely borne by the third-party brand merchants; and the customers shall be required to provide pictures, descriptions and other certification materials of the quality problems or wrong-problem products.
